About Us

CodeIQ Software Training Institute, located in Bhopal, is dedicated to bridging the gap between academia and industry. Our mission is to equip aspiring tech professionals with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the technology sector.

We offer comprehensive courses in Full Stack JAVA, Full Stack Python AIML, MERN Stack, MEAN Stack, and Data Analytics. Our experienced instructors provide personalized mentorship and practical training to ensure our students gain the necessary skills for their careers.

Our Courses

We have the most complete range of software training courses needed by the professionals around the world. Please do contact us for any queries related to the courses. Here are some of the popular courses we offer.

Full Stack Java Development

Master Java and become a Full Stack Developer. Learn to build scalable web applications using Java, Spring, and JavaScript. Gain hands-on experience with databases and front-end technologies like HTML and CSS.

Full Stack Python AIML

Dive into Python with a focus on AI and ML. Learn to develop intelligent applications using Python, TensorFlow, and JavaScript. Get hands-on experience with databases, HTML, and CSS.

MEAN & MERN Stack

Learn MongoDB, Express.js, Angular, React.js, and Node.js. Develop robust web applications using the MEAN & MERN stacks. Gain hands-on experience with databases and front-end technologies like HTML and CSS.

Data Analytics

Analyze data and draw meaningful insights. Learn to use Python and R for data analysis. Gain hands-on experience with databases and front-end technologies like HTML and CSS.
